Paris, France

Known as the "City of Light", Paris is renowned for its art, fashion, gastronomy, and culture. Famous attractions include the Eiffel Tower, Louvre Museum, and charming cafes lining the River Seine.

Rome, Italy

Rome, the capital of Italy, is a sprawling city with nearly 3,000 years of globally influential art, architecture, and culture. Key sites include the Colosseum, Roman Forum, and Vatican City.

Tokyo, Japan

Tokyo blends the ultramodern and the traditional, from neon-lit skyscrapers to historic temples. Don't miss the cherry blossoms in spring, the bustling Shibuya crossing, and authentic sushi.

New York, USA

Known as "The Big Apple", New York offers world-famous attractions such as Times Square, Central Park, the Statue of Liberty, and Broadway theaters. Experience the vibrant city life and diverse cuisine.
